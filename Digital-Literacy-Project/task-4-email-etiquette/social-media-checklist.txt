Responsible Social Media Use - Checklist

Do’s

• Think before you post (consider impact and audience) 
• Maintain a respectful and positive tone 
• Protect your personal information (privacy settings) 
• Share useful and credible content 
• Build a professional online presence 

 Don’ts 

• Don’t share sensitive personal details (address, phone, etc.) 
• Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content 
• Don’t spread fake news or unverified information 
• Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling 
• Don’t damage your digital reputation with careless posts
