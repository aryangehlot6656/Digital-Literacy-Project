Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students 



Prevention Tips



1. Use strong, unique passwords for each account (avoid using your name or DOB)
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on apps like email, social media, and banking
3. Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank
4. Always verify links before clicking (check spelling of websites and sender details)
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments
6. Keep your apps and devices updated to prevent security vulnerabilities
7. Download apps only from official app stores (Google Play Store / App Store)
8. Be careful about what you post—protect your personal and academic information


UPI / Financial Safety Tips



* Double-check UPI ID or QR code before sending money
* Never approve “collect requests” unless you trust the sender
* Do not fall for “reward” or “cashback” scams asking you to click links
* Immediately block your bank account if you suspect fraud
